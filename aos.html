AOS.

aOS is the core Arisen node daemon and it handles the blockchain data persistence layer, peer-to-peer networking and contract code scheduling. For development environments, aOS enables developers to setup a single node blockchain network. aOS offers a wide-range of features through plugins.

The RIX cryptocurrency is not for sale and is being given away for free here. The RIX value at the top of the this website reflects what RIX (ARISEN) is trading for on BitShares between those who own it. RIX is used to purchase computing resources on ARISEN and is traded by resource providers and buyers in that process. RIX is also earned by block producers in exchange for validating transactions.
